A Letter For You ❤️

Read this slowly. Every word comes straight from my heart.

Tanu,

You know what's the best thing about us since we have been together? Life has been interesting.

Now, you would probably ask, "How is that possible even after the break in our relationship?"

The answer is simple. Every single day has felt more exciting because somewhere in that day, I have thought about you. Regardless of our relationship status, regardless of how busy we are, regardless of our physical or emotional state... our hearts somehow always find a way to stay connected. Even on the busiest days, I wonder how my other half is doing. And somehow... that one thought alone makes life a little brighter.

I haven't had many opportunities to stand beside you and support you the way I wish I could. But I'd like to believe that even knowing I'm somewhere in the background of your mind gives you a little strength. Because that's exactly what happens to me. Just thinking about you pushes me to become better. To work harder. To keep going.

I hope you always cherish every birthday that comes your way. I hope that as your age increases, your happiness increases. Your confidence increases. Your dreams become bigger. And your success grows exponentially. Because if there's anyone who deserves every beautiful thing life has to offer... it's you.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for every memory. Every smile. Every laugh. Every hug. Every little moment that has unknowingly become one of the happiest parts of my life.

Happy Birthday, Doll ❤️

With all my love,
