Our Favourite Memories ❤️

Some memories never grow old. They simply become more precious with time.

🎁 The Handmade Cassette

Out of every gift I've ever received, nothing has ever touched my heart the way that handmade cassette did. Each page carried one of our memories. Each song matched that memory perfectly. I still remember sitting there while opening it, trying to hold back my emotions, and completely failing. I don't think you'll ever truly understand how much that gift meant to me.

😂 Two Heads. One Laugh.

One day we accidentally hit our heads together. Instead of checking ourselves, the very first thing we did was ask, "Are you okay?" Looking back... that's probably the most "us" thing we've ever done. And then we couldn't stop laughing.

❤️ Our Favourite Conversation

Me: Champ.

You: "I'm not your champ."
"I'm your baby."

Every single time I think about it, I smile like an idiot.

🌧️ Right Now

Life has become busier. Dreams have become bigger. Studies demand more from both of us. That's why we've chosen to take a small break. Not because we stopped caring. But because we care enough about each other's future. I genuinely believe this phase will pass. And when it does... we'll have even more memories waiting to be made.

☁️ A Promise

Years from now, I don't know where life will take us. But I hope that whenever you think about these moments, you smile the same way I do. Because for me, every memory with you has become one of my favourite places to revisit.
