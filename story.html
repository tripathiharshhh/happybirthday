Our Story ❤️

Every beautiful story starts with an ordinary moment. Ours started in the mountains.

🏔️ 16 January 2026

Manali. A college trip. Just another journey... or at least that's what I thought. If someone had told me that this trip would completely change my life, I would've laughed. Little did I know that destiny had already planned something beautiful.

🚌 17 January 2026

We met on the second day of the trip. Nothing dramatic happened. No movie-like scene. Just two people travelling together on the same bus. Looking back now, it's funny how such an ordinary meeting became one of the most important days of my life.

💬 18 January 2026

The next day... we finally had our first real conversation. Time disappeared. What started as a simple conversation somehow became hours of talking. Without even realizing it, I didn't want the conversation to end.

❤️ That Night

Later that evening... everyone somehow managed to squeeze onto one bed. There was barely any space left. Yet somehow... we ended up together in one tiny corner. Lying there. Hugging each other. While everyone else occupied almost the entire bed. That tiny little corner became my favourite place in the whole world. And somewhere in that moment... I quietly realised...

I like this girl.

☁️ Since Then

Life has never really been ordinary again. Every day became a little more exciting because somewhere in it... you were there.
